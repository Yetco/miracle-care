About Miracle Care Inc

Miracle Care Inc is an agency dedicated to serving the people of our community with compassion, dignity, and love. We are committed to making a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve.

We provide quality healthcare to improve the lives of individuals with intellectual and physical disabilities, regardless of income and social status. Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every person receives the care, respect, and support they deserve.

Under the leadership of our CEO/Executive Director, Vivian Ezirim, we strive to create miracles every day by enriching the lives of our patients through specialized care and unwavering acceptance.

Our Mission

  • Serve our community with compassion and dignity
  • Provide quality healthcare services
  • Improve lives of individuals with disabilities
  • Ensure care regardless of income or status

Our Values

  • Compassion in every interaction
  • Dignity and respect for all
  • Love and acceptance without judgment
  • Quality care as our standard
